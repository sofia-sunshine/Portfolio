danse macabre

(french: "dance of death")

Where art meets skin. Our shop is a unique blend of creativity, passion, and expertise. Our team of talented artists are dedicated to providing high-quality, custom tattoos that are tailored to meet your individual needs. We believe that getting a tattoo is a personal and meaningful experience, and our goal is to make sure that each and every one of our clients leaves with a piece of art they can be proud of.

We use only the highest quality inks and equipment, ensuring a safe and sterile environment for our clients. Our shop is open seven days a week and we offer appointments as well as walk-ins. We welcome clients of all ages and offer a wide range of styles, from traditional to contemporary.

At danse macabre, we are committed to providing a warm and friendly environment where our clients can feel comfortable and confident in their decision to get a tattoo. Whether you are looking for your first tattoo or your tenth, we are here to help.

Contact us today to book an appointment or stop by for a consultation.
